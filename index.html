Designed around the realities of project delivery

InScope is a suite of workflow apps designed around the realities of project delivery.

We think in terms of internal project logic, the external regulatory environment and team infrastructure — three connected dimensions that shape how the system works as a whole.

Each area contains focused apps that can be used independently or together, allowing teams to adapt the system to different project needs and levels of complexity.

Internal Direction

What are we trying to achieve?

Projects need direction before execution. Define success, structure teams, understand project context and shape delivery plans through connected tools for project definition and planning.

External Reality

how do we navigate the external regulatory environment?"

Projects operate within a wider environment of regulation, approvals and delivery constraints. Coordinate planning, compliance and external obligations alongside project delivery.

Team Infrastructure

How do teams work together to deliver it?

Delivery depends on visibility and shared understanding. Keep communication, updates and project information connected across the team environment.
